Accounts Receivable & Accounts Payable Management System										
Company										
PT Nusantara Office Supplies										
										
Objective										
Developed an Accounts Receivable (AR) and Accounts Payable (AP) Management System in Microsoft Excel to monitor invoices, customer payments, supplier payments, outstanding balances, and financial performance through an interactive dashboard.										
										
										
										
Features										
✅ Customer Master										
✅ Supplier Master										
✅ Sales Invoice										
✅ Customer Payment										
✅ Purchase Invoice										
✅ Supplier Payment										
✅ AR Aging Report										
✅ AP Aging Report										
✅ Dashboard										
										
Excel Skills										
-	SUMIFS									
-	IF									
-	TODAY									
-	VLOOKUP/XLOOKUP									
-	Data Validation									
-	Conditional Formatting									
-	Charts
